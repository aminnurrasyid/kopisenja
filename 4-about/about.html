ABOUT US

Perfect moment, perfect coffee.

Imagine having a cup of coffee with your loved ones, relaxing on a chair facing the sea, while waiting for the sun to set. Incredible, isn't it? This true story has been an inspiration for us in starting this business. We have observed the astonishing moment of an old couple chatting and laughing while waiting for the night to cover the sky, and steam coming out from 2 cups of coffees.


Huge success comes from small insight.

It all started with a cup of coffee. Having a first sip truly soothe my feelings. I wonder how it does to everyone else? Since then, I started to share my experience with my close friends. Then, one of us come out with an idea to share our happiness with our community. Nearly 60 days, we finally reserved a place to bring out the happiness to others. In the duration of almost 2 years, we have expanded our marketing section according to the technology trends by developing our own website to 'espresso' our moments to all over the world.
